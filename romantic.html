I Promise

Date: Wednesday, June 12, 2013 I promise...... Oh love of mine, My eyes are searching for you So that, I could promise you… That I’d be there Whenever you need me I’d care, Either you’d care or not care. I apologize, Because I may not give you stars and moon. But I’d ensure, That I’d give my shoulders, To soak your tears…. When you’d want to share I’d be there with my ears I may not be able to dive into the ocean, To get precious gems for you. But I’d lie down, When you need to walk in the path of thorns. I’ll show the stars and say, You’re giving hard times to the stars, As you shine more….. You’re giving jealousy to the moon, As she has scars in her… You sometimes make the sun cry, Because you are warmer, You sometimes make breeze stop, Because you’re cooler….. Whenever I find myself Alone and sad You come and ask You're the number one who makes me glad So dearest love of mine I promise, That I’d be with you Forever and ever… Till my eyes get closed And I sleep in the bed of afterlife…….

For an Angel

Date: Tuesday, June 04, 2013 I have been treated like a fool, Treated mean and cruel My faithful heart was broken, And torn all apart. But it all ended And came a sunshine Who holds me tight And tells everything’s fine. Every night I prayed to the stars, In the noon I prayed the sun I even worshipped clouds and the moon And wondered…where the mighty god is hidden. When I saw the smile of yours, Light as air became my heart. The tears of joy rolled down. Oh! What a moment it was. My need of god was over As so angelicious are you. My fate of emptiness is over As so precious are you. The time has come when, I can see the smile of yours Talk about how great are you Utter the words of appreciations Thank you for the smiley art you drew Which took my sorrows apart…. And took me top in the chart Of the graph of joy………..

Us Together

Date: Thursday, December 4, 2025 A bright summer morning, Colorful skies — Those rainbows of dreams. The whole world feels brighter Whenever I see you smile. The wind moves through me, A dream come true Whenever you’re near. That feeling when I hold your hand — Not even the stars compare to it. That feeling when I pull you close — Not even the stars compare to this. A handful of joy, Yet now I hold so much more As long as you’re by my side. When I open my eyes, You’re the first one I see; Before I close them, You’re the last. I wait for that bright morning When you’re beside me, Again and forever. Through thick and thin, We walk together All our lives — Till death do us part.

A Try

Date: June, 12, 2013

When stars are hidden In those dark clouds When the little droplets of rain Breaks down in my face, I miss you, I recall my days, Those past days Where we were holding Each other’s back Where we were those free birds Of same pack… I miss those days Where you and I used to roam In the sky of charm, freedom And friendship I miss those days Where you cared me, You made me feel special… Even if I did not have anything So special… When those rain drops Break down into many I remember how we Got apart And miss you Every time when First week of august comes When I get victory When no one care about my identity… Or when I feel pity On myself… You are the person Who comes first in my mind And say everything is better A new way to happiness You’ll find…
